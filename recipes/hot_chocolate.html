Hot Delicious Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1–2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1–2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • ¼ cup chopped dark chocolate (for richness)
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Warm the milk on low–medium heat (don’t boil).
  2. Add cocoa powder + sugar → whisk well (no lumps).
  3. Add chopped chocolate → stir until melted and smooth.
  4. Add vanilla (optional) → mix and serve hot.

Quick Tips

  • Use dark chocolate for richer taste
  • Add a pinch of salt → enhances flavor
  • For thicker texture → simmer a bit longer
