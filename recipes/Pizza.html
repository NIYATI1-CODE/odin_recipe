Pizza

Image from LBB

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour (00 flour if possible)
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • ½ tsp yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Tomato sauce (simple crushed tomatoes + salt)
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix water + yeast → add flour + salt → knead till smooth.
  2. Cover and let it rise for 6–8 hours (or overnight for best flavor).
  3. Stretch by hand (don’t use rolling pin) into a thin round.Stretch by hand (don’t use rolling pin) into a thin round.
  4. Spread tomato sauce → add mozzarella → drizzle olive oil.
  5. Bake at highest temperature (250–300°C) for 7–10 min(or 90 seconds in a super-hot oven/OTG if possible).
  6. Add fresh basil on top after baking.

Quick Tips

  • Keep toppings minimal (that’s the real Neapolitan style)
  • Slightly burnt spots = good (authentic “leopard” crust)
  • Softer center, airy edges = perfect result
